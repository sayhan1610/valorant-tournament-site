      About the tournament

      Join our Valorant tournament and experience the benefits of esports and teamwork. Esports are not only fun and exciting, but also help you develop skills such as communication, collaboration, strategy, and problem-solving. Playing Valorant with other players also helps you build friendships, community, and school spirit. Whether you are a beginner or a pro, you will find a place in our tournament.


      The tournament will take place on 16th February in the school computer lab. You can register as a team of five players, or as an individual and we will assign you to a team. The registration fee is 100 turkish lira per player, which will cover the prize, the hotspot, and a donation to a charity of our choice. Each player is also allowed to bring their own gears including laptops, keyboards, mice & headphones.


      The tournament format is a knockout, with four teams playing against each other in swiftplays (first to 5 rounds). The winning team will advance to the final, where they will play a full match (first to 13 rounds) against the other finalist. The final match will be streamed live in the conference hall, where you can watch it for a small fee of 50 turkish lira. The winning team will receive a battle pass as a reward, and the remaining money will go to charity.


      If you are new to Valorant, or want to learn more about the game, you can join our agent guides session, where we will explain the different agent roles and abilities. The session will be held a week before the tournament, on 9th February in the school computer lab.


      To register for the tournament, or for any questions or feedback, please fill out this online form or contact Sayhan at sayhanrahman@gokkusagi.k12.tr or Instagram/Discord at alexx_sh16. We hope to see you at the tournament and have a great time playing Valorant!
